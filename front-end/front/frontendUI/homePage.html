About Us

Center Research Technology Development abbreviated as CRTD aims to provide quality education to all children at elementary level. Build a society committed to constitutional values with the help of quality education and literacy. In order to make the dream of education a reality for every deserving student in the country, an initiative by government is taken to ensure the upliftment of every section of the society. Bring equity to the system of secondary education by including disadvantaged groups as well as weaker sections. Ensure equality and improved standards of education by supporting the existing institutions and facilitating, setting up of new ones.

Know Your Child Performance

CRTD gives you the current status and student related updates. Through this feature , one can know what is their child's weakness and work on that. CRTD keeps an academic record of students through which he/she can improve the required skills.

More Competetion

This platform provides a competitive environment to make the students future ready. It trains them to compete with a wide range of students and work on themselves. CRTD gives a clear-cut idea of where the students are lagging behind and what all improvements are need to be done.

Improve Your Weaknesses

A well said quote which goes by "Failures are stepping stones to success" which in real life turns out to be true. Knowing your weaknesses and working upon them is the biggest improvement one can do. CRTD helps you to overcome your problems and analyse them in a detailed way.
